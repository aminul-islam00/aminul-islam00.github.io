About Me

A highly qualified and experienced Corporate Trainer & Psychological Counsellor, specializing in Train the Trainer, leadership and management and a range of personal and professional development skills. Md. Aminul Islam is a postgraduate qualified Educator, adult education Specialist and Mental health expert that has delivered training to over 130,000 students in different organizations globally. Md. Islam brings you the assurance of over 20 years of experience designing, developing and delivering training to corporates, government, educational institutions, nonprofits and businesses in all industry sectors - with significant experience in facilitation and people development in the Pharmaceuticals & FMCG selling, resources, oil & gas and civil infrastructure industries in Bangladesh and in abroad. Qualified as a frontline Management Trainer-Assessor, Md. Islam specializes in facilitating ‘Train the Trainer’ programs, workforce development training and leadership and management training (as well as a range of personal and professional development topics) to new staff, right through to management, executive leadership and Board Directors via online, face-to-face and blended delivery. Md. Islam has held executive positions at the Olympic Industries Limited, A T Haque Limited and Pharmasia Limited, holding the record for being the successful Trainer and Psychological counsellor in corporate history and leading the companies in Bangladesh through a successful development growth and ranking the respectively at number 1 in the country at the time of his leadership. His experience also includes Head of Training positions, and was previously the ‘Asst. Manager’ for the National Pharma Industry Skills for the Marketing, Sales and Wan Group LLc. Doha, Qatar. As well as a Workforce Development Consultant for medium and large organizations. Md. Islam practical experience in frontline and executive roles, as well as theoretical expertise and qualifications, make her a highly dynamic, knowledgeable and professional Corporate Trainer and Psychological counsellor for organizations looking to add further capability and capacity to their training team.
